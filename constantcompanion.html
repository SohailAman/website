Constant Companion

Creative Direction
Constant Companion is a voice-assistant technology package to enrich the lives of older adults through the use of smart speakers that keep loved ones engaged, connected and protected.

We created a series of print and digital campaigns to help promote and inspire the sales teams to do their best work, acquire the best talent, and enhance the lives of millions of senior adults who needed 'care that's always there.'

Target Audience: Healthcare Facility Owners, Hospitals, Doctors with Elderly Patients with a focus of helping nurses and doctors become more virtual and availble to optimize their time

Deliverables

Print & Illustations

Curated product and box designs, created a series of B2C manuals on how to set up the hardware including a custom icon library set, product catalogs and pricing sheets and more

Websites & Videos

Stood up promotional Wix landing pages for promotional content, storyboarded and designed promotional product videos as well as a creating an enticing video reel for their careers page

Presentations & Trade Show Materials

Delivered pitch decks and presentation templates, promotional materials like: postcards, business cards, brochures and drafted trade show booth designs

Constant Companion sells their personalized companion software embedded in products like the Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant speakers. This is primarily for healthcare facility partnerships so we crafted two product packages for both B2C and B2B audiences. We created start-up guides for our end users, and product catalogs for the sales teams' pitch to business clients.
