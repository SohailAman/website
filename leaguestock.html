League Stock

Creative Direction
LeagueStock is a fantasy sports platform that's set up like the stock market, but with a twist: instead of investing in companies, users can invest in talented pro athletes and earn big bucks in the process.

Captivate is a partner agency with MXMD that collaborates on a variety of cutting-edge products and designs. MXMD was contracted to lead the creative direction of the brand, design the app and website, and lead the art direction of their social media and advertising efforts.

Target Audience: Sports enthusiasts ready for the next big thing (and next big athlete) with at least a basic knowledge of the stock market
In Partnership with Capitvate

Deliverables

Logo & Branding

Produced a new logo and logo elements based on our user findings that were more fantasy sports related

App & Website

Creative directed and designed the full mobile application for iOS and Android as well as create a new website design and a few landing pages

Social Media & More
