Survey Monkey + Momentive

UI/UX + Art Directon
Brand strategists at Momentive reached out to MXMD to help with the creative merger of SurveyMonkey and Momentive. We needed to tackle a variety of tasks, from launching a new corporate blog called ReShape, to expanding on their exisitng design direction, to assisting with crafting customer story case studies landing pages for clients like the Golden State Warriors and LG.
2021-2022

Deliverables

Blog UX/UI

Created a blog experience that delivers on their new brand narrative, relays value, and delivers reusable Figma components to account for all the needs of a solid blog resouce

Web Pages

Produced additional internal Momentive web pages, new imagery direction, and graphics that matched with their new aesthetic for resources, case studies, and more

Data Visualization

Crafted a reusable library with a visual design that is in line with the new parent brand identity with charts and graphs that are provide solutions for various levels of data

The Blog

Momementive had an idea to "Elevate stories that matter", with this in mind, we wanted to create a blog that inspired our upmarket buyers and felt grounded in reliable information with "research, insights, and expert opinions to help [their clients] reshape [their] products, industry, and the experiences".

Additional Web Pages

As a secondary project, MXMD worked on additional pages for Momentive as well as defining a image composite library to maintain the new art direction proposed. We worked hand in hand with the creative lead for SurveyMonkey to help build on their existing components to creating more pages and more content for their growing parent site and blog.

Data Visualization

Data Visualization is changing the way the public digests information. Together, we set out to create a data visualization library for the company to deliver relevant and meaningful data insights that is consistent across the board.
